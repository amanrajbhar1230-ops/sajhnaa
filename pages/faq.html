Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our jewellery, orders, and more.

Is your jewellery real gold/silver?▼

Our jewellery is premium artificial/fashion jewellery. We use high-quality alloys with gold plating, rhodium plating, and CZ stones that look and feel like real jewellery at a fraction of the price.

How do I care for my jewellery?▼

Store in a dry, airtight pouch or box. Avoid contact with water, perfume, and chemicals. Wipe with a soft dry cloth after wearing. Remove before sleeping, bathing, or exercising.

What is your return policy?▼

We offer a 7-day easy return policy. If you're not satisfied, initiate a return from your Orders page. The item must be unused and in original packaging. Refunds are processed within 5 business days.

How long does delivery take?▼

Metro cities: 2-3 business days. Other cities: 4-5 business days. Remote areas: 5-7 business days. Free shipping on orders above ₹999.

Do you offer gift wrapping?▼

Yes! All orders come in our signature Shivani gift box at no extra cost. Premium gift wrapping with a personalized message is available for ₹99.

Can I cancel my order?▼

You can cancel orders that are in "Pending" status from your Orders page. Once an order is packed or shipped, it cannot be cancelled but you can return it after delivery.

Are your products skin-friendly?▼

Yes! Our jewellery is nickel-free and hypoallergenic. However, if you have sensitive skin, we recommend doing a patch test first.
